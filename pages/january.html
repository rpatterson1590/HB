From the Pastor’s Desk

It Pays To Serve The Lord

It Is Not Over Till The Lord Says It Is Over!

Have you been discouraged? Have you lost your FAITH in believing you are going to be okay? Never forget who your Heavenly Father is! By accepting Jesus Christ as your Lord and Savior, YOU became a Child Of God!!! Your Father is watching over you and listening to your ever cry. You are never alone. He Cares, and He Loves YOU!!! You must always remember, that God created you, and He can fix you, at all times in whatever arises in your life. This month we celebrate FATHER’S DAY. Some of our earthly fathers have passed away, but we know our Heavenly Father will always be here to assure us we are never alone. If you are hurting, in pain, sad, depressed, in need, troubled, lonely, or given a bad report NEVER GIVE UP!!! Your Heavenly Father God will give you strength, love, direction, help, and healing. NEVER believe the lies of the devil as he tries to make you believe God does not care, and lying saying nothing will never change. Never listen to negative and hurtful people that are not there to build your Faith Stronger In God. God Will Make Away Where There Seem To Be No Way! Keep Praising, Trusting, and Obeying God Everyday and See how God will turn your trial into a blessing! Your Heavenly Father God can use your situation for His Glory, to be seen by others to come to believe in Him. What A Testimony You Will Be Given, As God Turns Your Life Around, To An Healing Experience! PRAISE THE LORD!!!

Love, Pastor Debbie
