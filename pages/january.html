March 2026

Here we are in Month # 3 in 2026. Already CHANGE is taking place, and it is all good!!! Next Week Sunday, March 8, TIME CHANGES To Daylight Savings Time!!!! Please Remember: WE ARE MOVING OUR CLOCKS FORWARD! On March 20 will be our first day of SPRING!!! Thank You Jesus!!!! Those Cold Winter Months Were Terrible! I Love Spring!!! The signs of NEW BEGINNINGS! The weather and air has that feeling of newness and joy to be outside to take in the beauty of the grass turning green, the trees are budding, flowers are blooming, and gardens are being planted and the seeds that were planted in the ground, turn into live plants above the ground to soon be vegetables to be harvested. What had looked dull or dead during the winter months, was now showing to be alive and producing again on trees and plants!!! Let’s Also Come ALIVE!!! Let’s start today to SPRING FORWARD in our relationship with God. Let’s get up and pray more, read our Bibles more, Praise God everyday more, let’s talk about the Love & Goodness of God more, let’s attend more than just one church service weekly, let’s be faithful servants to give of our tithes and offerings to help take care of the needs of God’s House, let’s help paint & repair the areas of God’s House that we know needs to be fixed, let’s invite and bring new visitors to church with us, let’s be a more compassionate Christian to care for the widows, the sick, & needy, and let’s be a TRUE SERVANT OF GOD that one day Jesus will say to us when we get to Heaven: “WELL DONE, GOOD FAITHFUL SERVANT; THOU HAST BEEN FAITHFUL OVER A FEW THINGS, I WILL MAKE THEE RULER OVER MANY: ENTER THOU INTO THE JOY OF THE LORD!” (St. Matthew 25: 23)
