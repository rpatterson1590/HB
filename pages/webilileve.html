Our Statement of Faith

What We Believe

At Heaven Bound Church of God, our faith is rooted in the Word of God, the saving power of Jesus Christ, the work of the Holy Spirit, and the blessed hope of His return.

Built on God’s Word

These beliefs guide our worship, teaching, ministry, and daily walk with Christ. Each statement is supported by Scripture and points back to the truth, grace, and holiness of God.

1

The Bible

We believe in the verbal inspiration of the Bible and accept God’s Word as the foundation for doctrine, correction, instruction, and Christian living.

  • 2 Timothy 3:16
2

One God

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: the Father, the Son, and the Holy Ghost.

  • 1 John 5:7
3

Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus Christ is the only begotten Son of God, born of the Virgin Mary, crucified, risen from the dead, and ascended into heaven.

  • John 3:16
  • Matthew 1:23
  • Luke 23:33
  • Luke 24:6–7
  • Acts 1:9
4

Repentance

We believe all have sinned and must repent, turning to God for forgiveness, cleansing, and restoration.

  • Romans 3:23
  • Luke 13:3
  • Acts 3:19
5

Salvation and New Birth

We believe salvation and the new birth come through faith in the blood of Jesus Christ, by grace and through the redeeming work of the Lord.

  • Romans 5:9
  • Ephesians 1:7
  • John 3:3
  • John 1:12–13
6

Sanctification

We believe sanctification follows the new birth and is worked in the believer through the Word of God and the Spirit of God.

  • John 17:17
  • 1 Thessalonians 4:3
  • 2 Thessalonians 2:13
7

Holiness

We believe holiness is God’s standard for His people and that believers are called to live in purity, obedience, and reverence before the Lord.

  • Hebrews 12:14
  • 1 Peter 1:15–16
  • 2 Corinthians 7:1
8

The Holy Ghost

We believe in the baptism of the Holy Ghost after a clean heart, and that the Father gives the Spirit to those who ask Him.

  • Acts 15:8–9
  • Acts 2:38
  • Luke 11:13
9

Speaking in Tongues

We believe speaking in tongues is the initial evidence of the baptism of the Holy Ghost, as shown in the New Testament church.

  • Acts 2:4
  • Acts 10:44–46
  • Acts 19:6
10

Water Baptism

We believe in water baptism by immersion in the name of the Father, the Son, and the Holy Ghost as an outward testimony of faith.

  • Matthew 28:19
  • Romans 6:4
  • Acts 8:38
11

Divine Healing

We believe in divine healing through the Atonement and in the power of prayer for the sick.

  • Isaiah 53:5
  • Matthew 8:16–17
  • James 5:14–15
12

Communion and Feet Washing

We believe in the Lord’s Supper and in the washing of the saints’ feet as acts of remembrance, humility, and service.

  • Luke 22:19–20
  • 1 Corinthians 11:24–26
  • John 13:14–15
13

The Second Coming

We believe in the premillennial second coming of Jesus Christ and look forward to His return with hope and expectation.

  • 1 Thessalonians 4:16–17
  • Revelation 20:4–6
  • Acts 1:11
14

Eternity

We believe in eternal life for the righteous and eternal punishment for the wicked, according to the teaching of Scripture.

  • Matthew 25:46
  • John 5:28–29
  • Daniel 12:2
