What Sundays Look Like

Growing in faith, friendship, and worship

🍽️

Breakfast

  • A hot, healthy breakfast is served before service.
  • Children’s Church workers help make sure no child goes hungry.
  • Breakfast is served in the classroom in a caring environment.
🎶

Worship & Music

  • Children are taught worship songs during Children’s Church.
  • Kids form the Heaven Bound Church Youth Choir.
  • The youth choir sings in the main worship service on select Sundays.
📖

Teaching & Activities

  • Weekly Bible lessons help children understand God’s Word.
  • Memory verses are included to strengthen each lesson.
  • Craft time is often used to reinforce what children are learning.
🎄

Prayer & Christmas Season

  • Children learn about the power of prayer each week.
  • Dedicated prayer time is part of Children’s Church.
  • From September through December, children rehearse for the Christmas play.
