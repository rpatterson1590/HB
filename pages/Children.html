Heaven Bound Church of God

Children’s Church

Our Children’s Church is a safe, welcoming, and joyful place where children ages 3–12 can learn about God’s love through breakfast, worship, Bible lessons, crafts, music, prayer, and special seasonal activities.

What We Do Meet The Team

Sunday Morning

A loving place for children to learn, worship, and grow.

Ages

3–12

Children’s Church is designed for kids ages 3 through 12.

When

Sunday Morning

Children meet during Sunday morning service for lessons and activities.

Purpose

Grow In Faith

Kids are taught God’s Word in a way they can understand and enjoy.

What We Do

Every part of Children’s Church is designed to help kids feel loved, included, and excited about Jesus.

🍳
Breakfast

Breakfast Before Service

A hot, healthy breakfast is served in the classroom before service. We want every child to feel cared for, welcomed, and ready for the morning.

🎵
Worship & Music

Children’s Worship

Children learn songs and have opportunities to be part of the Heaven Bound Church Youth Choir, singing in the main worship service on select Sundays.

📖
Bible Lessons

God’s Word For Kids

Each week includes a Bible lesson and memory verse to help children learn Scripture and understand how to live for Jesus.

🎨
Activities

Crafts & Learning

Craft time is often included to reinforce the weekly Bible lesson in a fun, creative, and hands-on way.

🙏
Prayer

Learning To Pray

Children learn the power of prayer and have dedicated prayer time each week as they grow in their relationship with God.

⭐
Christmas Season

Christmas Play Practice

From September through December, children participate in weekly Christmas play rehearsals during Children’s Church.
