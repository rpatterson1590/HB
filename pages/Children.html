Church Highlights

Stay connected with Children’s Church, worship, teaching, and ministry moments.

Breakfast

Every child is welcomed and cared for.

  • A hot, healthy breakfast is served in the classroom before service.
  • Children’s Church workers help make sure no child goes hungry.
  • Breakfast gives children a warm and friendly start to Sunday morning.
Teaching & Activities

Bible lessons made clear for young hearts.

  • Weekly Bible lessons include memory verses.
  • Craft time often reinforces the lesson of the day.
  • Children are encouraged to learn, ask questions, and grow in faith.
Worship & Prayer

Kids worship, sing, pray, and participate.

  • Children learn worship songs during Children’s Church.
  • The youth choir sings in the main worship service on select Sundays.
  • Dedicated prayer time helps children learn the power of prayer.

Children’s Ministry

From breakfast to Bible lessons, every Sunday is designed to help children feel loved, included, and pointed toward Jesus.

Worship & Music

Sister Marie Lashley teaches songs, and the children form the Heaven Bound Church Youth Choir.

Prayer

Children learn about the power of prayer and have dedicated prayer time each week.

Christmas Season

From September through December, children rehearse weekly for the Christmas play.
